Cinderella

A kind, loving and inquisitive girl is locked in endless servitude by her wicked step-mother.

Key Interactions

  • Opens the show: happy with the other villages
  • Get's ordered about by her step-family
  • Doesn't understand Button's means her when he sings "find me somebody to love"
  • Get's locked in the cellar as punishment for making the dress she was making for the ball
  • Fairy raises her hopes, frees her from the cellar and sends her to the ball
  • Meets Prince Charming at the ball
  • Confuses her step-family. Why is Cinderella so happy?
  • Tries on the slipper, and it fits! Marries Pricne Charming
