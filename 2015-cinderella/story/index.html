Story Overview

Read on for an overview of our Cinderella story

Act One

Village Square

On A Wonderful Day Like Today

Cinderella & Villagers dance in the mid-day sun

The villagers leave and Cinderella chats to the audience, she's trying to find her friend Buttons. Buttons arrives as Cinderella leaves.

To Love Somebody

Buttons sings of his unrequited love for Cinderella

Cinderella reappears. Buttons builds up the courage to finally tell Cinderella he loves her. They're interrupted by Camilla and Drusilla.

After a brief exchange Cinderella and Buttons leave.

Sisters

Camilla & Drusilla sing of their relationship

Cinderella's House

When her step-sisters return home Cinderella lays out the dinner. They work Cinderella ragged and it's clear life for her is very hard.

The dressmaker and jeweller arrive with fine wares. Cinderella get's nothing. Baroness preempts Cinderella's sadness by shouting at her.

Once Upon A Dream

Cinderella remembers her father's dream

The Palace

We learn about Charming's dreams, and his (lack of) musical abilities. Dandini is about to tell Charming about the ball when...

Queen and King arrive, followed by their noisy advisers. Queen announces Charming will go to ball to find a bride. Charming is annoyed!

Charming plans to write a letter to his future bride expressing his real feelings. Dandini helps by dictating suggested lines, but is comically misinterpreted when he gets into a scuffle with a footman.

Back at Cinderella's House

Cinderella is cleaning when Buttons bursts in. He brings news of invitations to the royal ball. They plan to make a dress so Cinderella can attend. The conversation turns to love:

Somebody To Love

They both need somebody to love but Cinderella doesn't know Buttons wants her!

When Baroness, Camilla & Drusilla return home early, Buttons hides in the kitchen. Cinderella is sent out to fetch water for Baroness' bath when Royal Mail arrives with the invitations.

Cinderella is missing her chance! As Royal Mail is about to leave Buttons errupts from the kitchen to get her an extra ticket. Buttons shouts Cinderella (who's outside) with the audiences help.

Baroness tricks Cinderella into thinking she can go to the ball. When left alone Cinderella sings a hopeful tune:

Please, Please, Please Let Me Get What I Want

Good times for a change! Cinderella pleads with the universe to let her get what she wants this time!

The Pallace Balcony

The night before the ball Charming and Dandini have a disagreement. Dandini play acts a date as the girl which ends comically.

But things turn sour when Dandini brings up Queen and King. Dandini leaves and Charming feels alone and frustrated.

Please, Please, Please Let Me Get What I Want

In this short reprise Charming echos Cinderella's wish, despite their different circumstances.

Cinderella's House, Day of the Ball

Camilla & Drusilla get ready to go out whilst Cinderella waits on their every need. Baroness finds out about Cinderella making the dress and swells with anger. Camilla & Drusilla tear up the dress!

Welcome Home

The dark and distored style of this song represents Baroness as she screams at Cinderella.

Baroness, Camilla & Drusilla leave to attend the ball. Cinderella sobs locked in the cellar. Fairy arrives to save the day!

When You Believe

This uplifting number inspires Cinderella as Fariy magics up a carriage for her journey.
