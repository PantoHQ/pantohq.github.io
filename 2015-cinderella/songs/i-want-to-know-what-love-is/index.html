I Want To Know What Love Is (Medley)

Foreigner Charming & Cinderella
  • Easy Music
  • 2 Vocalists
  • Some Harmony
  • Complex Timing

Charming lets Cinderella know he wants to know what love is. Fairy embues him with magical musical powers to make him good. The song is going well, but suddenly Fairy's spell goes awry. The song keeps skipping to other tracks and the magic forces Charming to sing.

Songs include: "I Want To Know What Love Is" (Foreigner), "Someone Like You" (Adele), "Paparazzi" (Lady GaGa), "I'm Yours" (Jason Mraz), "Wrecking Ball" (Miley Cyrus), "With Or Without You" (U2), "Say Something" (A Great Big World), "Wherever You Will Go" (The Calling), "Can You Feel The Love Tonight" (Lion King), "Man In The Mirror" (Michael Jackson), "Let It Be" (The Beatles), "Take On Me" (A-ha), "Let It Go" (Frozen), and again finally "I Want To Know What Love Is" (Foreigner).
