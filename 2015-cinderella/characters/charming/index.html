Charming

The prince rebels against his make-belive life in his search for sanity.
  • ~ 200 Lines
  • 6 Lead Vocal Parts

Charming struggles with his princely responsibilities. He's not particularly smart, but he tries really hard to make a difference. He isn't an especially witty character but instead represents the emotional drive of the panto.

Key Moments

  • Resists his parents wish to find a wife at the ball
  • Writes a comically inappropriate letter to his future wife
  • Discusses his worries with Dandini
  • At the ball he instantly notices Cinderella and falls in love
  • Serenades Cinderella
  • Sings at the royal court, convincing them to search for Cinderella
  • Tries the slipper on Cinderella and proposes marriage
